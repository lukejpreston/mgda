About Melanie

The principal of MGDA, Melanie has been dancing since she was two years old. Melanie has qualifications from the AAD as well as GCSEs and A Level in Dance, she also has a BA(Hons) in Dance, Performance and Teaching from UCLan. She has returned from London after teaching dance for over a year to bring all her knowledge back home to The Lakes
